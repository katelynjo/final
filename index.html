Growth

Where did it all start?

  • 2019-2020

    Experimenting

    During these years, I decided to pick up a new hobby of doing nails. In high school, I always wanted my nails done for school dances and quickly found out how expensive they were. I then decided to learn how to do it myself.

    Practice and Skill

    During this time, I began to practice nails on myself. Through trial and a lot of error, I started to gain my footing in the trade. I have only ever been self taught so there is so much more to learn and explore as I continue to grow.

    Putting it into Motion

    I began to do nails for my sisters and would practice doing it on a hand other than my own. I was also able to collect many materials and supplies needed for nails thoughout this year. 2022 was crucial for my learning and the perfecting of my skills.

  • 2023

    From Hobby to Career

    This year has been huge for the growth of my little business. I began to take clients and started to receive income from what used to be a hobby. I now plan on getting certified this summer as a nail technician! I cannot wait to see where my journey takes me.

Nail Polish

Simple yet elegant.

Using nail polish is always a safe choice at a nail salon. It makes for a very simple removal by wiping it off with acetone or nail polish removal when needed. It does require hand drying which can be time consuming. Other than that, nail polish is a great option that I offer at my salon!

Gel Nails

Strong and Sturdy

Gel nails are so amazing and versitile. They cure under a UV lamp, and after being cured, they are fully dry. They are also a great option if you are wanting an added design. It works well to layer different designs and paints!

Structured Gel

Great for Growth.

Structured gel is a thicker version of gel nail polish and is great for forming a structure. Using the structured gel as a base is a great way for your natural nail to gain some length. If you are looking for a way to grow out your nails to be long and natural, then structured gel is for you!

Gel-X

Long and Confident

Gel-X is a fool proof way to gain quick length with a smooth face of the nail. It adds that extra length which is great for wanting to add more space for more intricate designs. It is a quick and flawless method to creating, long, strong and beautiful nails.

Acrylic Nails

The Art of Nails

Acrylic nails are done by wetting a brush with a liquid called monomer and then dipping that damp brush into an acrylic powder. It then creates an acrylic bead which can be used to form a nail. It hardens into the shape it is put in after about a minute. Acrylics are such a fun way to show off lengthy and bold nails.

Nail Removal

The most important part of nail care.

Nail removal is a service that I offer because it is so important to take care of your nails. If nails are removed incorrectly, it can severly damage your nail, leaving it in worse condition than before you painted them. Nail removal is a crucial step in nail care.
